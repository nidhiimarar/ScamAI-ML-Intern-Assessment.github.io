WakeyBrain: An adaptation of _Flappy Bird_ that teaches users how to use AI responsibly!
Click on this link to play (you can also find in the "About" section of this repo): https://nidhiimarar.github.io/ScamAI-ML-Intern-Assessment.github.io/

Through this game, people will be exposed to examples of ethical AI usage as well as be forced to exercise critical thinking skills (which overusage of AI can limit).
You have to continuously press space to keep the player from crashing into the ground and dying. Each pillar will have two gaps through which you can pass, but each gap will contain a box that displays either an ethical usage of an AI or an unethical usage. If you pass through a box that demonstrates unethical usage of AI, the water level in the background will decrease. If the water level reaches 0, you lose. You can increase the water level by passing through options that show ethical usage of AI.
